GenAI Red-Teaming for SMEs – Business Plan

1. Executive Summary

This business plan outlines the GenAI Red-Teaming service targeting Swiss and DACH SMEs deploying RAG bots and generative AI systems. The service addresses critical risks such as prompt injection, data leakage, context bleed, and compliance gaps.

  • Target Customers: Swiss SMEs (1–250 employees) without in-house AI security expertise
  • Business Model: Solution Provider (audit) + Subscription (monitoring) + Add-On Services
  • Financial Goal: Break-even is targeted within 3 years, scaling to 25+ audits/year by year 5

Service Offerings & Pricing Overview

  • One-time initial audit: CHF 5,000
  • Subscription-based monitoring: CHF 1,500/month (CHF 18,000/year)
  • Add-on services: custom workshops, tool deployment, retainer consulting

2. Market & Opportunity

2.1 Market Size & Growth

  • Swiss AI market projected CHF 1.6 Billion by 2033 (21.2% CAGR)
  • SME AI adoption growing from 22% to 34% (2024–2025)
  • Swiss cybersecurity market estimated USD 1.5 billion (2024) for MSSP/MDR segment

2.2 Target Segment & Pain Points

SMEs represent >99% of Swiss companies. Their primary pain points include:

  • Risk of confidential/internal data exposure
  • Lack of AI governance frameworks
  • Compliance/regulatory pressure
  • Cost/complexity of hiring AI security experts

2.3 Competitive Landscape & Differentiation

Competitors include traditional cybersecurity firms, MSSPs, and AI vendors with security modules.

Unique Value Proposition:

  • Hyper-specialization in RAG vulnerabilities
  • Compliance focus tailored to Swiss/DACH law
  • Cost-effective fixed-scope audits leveraging automation tools
