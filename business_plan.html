GenAI Red Teaming for SMEs

Preventing Data Leakage in RAG Bots (Switzerland Focus)

1. Overview & Idea

A specialized cybersecurity service for Swiss SMEs deploying Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) bots and other GenAI systems. The service conducts automated red-teaming audits to detect prompt injections, data leakage risks, and compliance gaps.

  • Founder: Naveen Anpalagan
  • Market: SMEs in Switzerland and DACH region
  • Focus Period: 2025–2029
  • Goal: Provide affordable, expert-level GenAI red-teaming and continuous monitoring for SMEs without in-house AI security expertise.
